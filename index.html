Frequently asked questions

How can I place an order?

You can select books by adding them to your cart, and then proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and other options depending on your region.

How long will it take to process my order?

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. You will receive a notification when your order has been shipped..

What is the shipping cost?

The shipping cost is calculated based on your location and the total weight of the order. You will be able to see the full cost at the checkout stage.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number at the email address you provided.
